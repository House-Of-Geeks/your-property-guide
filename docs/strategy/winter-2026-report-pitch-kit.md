# Winter 2026 data report — journalist pitch kit

**The asset:** https://www.yourpropertyguide.com.au/guides/cheapest-suburbs-australian-capitals-winter-2026
**Goal:** followed editorial links from AU news/property outlets. The domain has 8 referring domains (all nofollow, spam-score 50). Links are the binding constraint on every ranking the site has — nothing else moves positions 4–20 into the top 3 until this does.
**Effort:** ~30–45 min to send the first 10 pitches. Send from andy@theandylife.com, plain text, no attachments (link only).

## The hooks (lead with ONE per pitch)

1. **National:** Across all eight greater capitals, only **8 verified suburbs are left under $500k** — five of them in Darwin. Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide and Canberra: zero.
2. **Sydney:** Not one verified Sydney suburb has a median under $700k — or $800k. The floor is **Macquarie Fields at $859,000**. Penrith is $932,000.
3. **Adelaide (strongest single-city angle):** Australia's famously affordable capital now has **zero suburbs under $600k**. The Elizabeth suburbs — sub-$300k within living memory — now sit at $640k–$690k on the SA government's own quarterly figures.
4. **Melbourne contrarian:** Melbourne is now the cheapest big east-coast capital to enter — 13 verified suburbs under $600k, all in the west/north corridors (Melton $510k).
5. **Regional escape:** A house in **Broken Hill (pop. ~18,000) costs $190k** — about a fifth of Sydney's cheapest suburb. Charleville QLD is $164k.

## Why a journalist should care about *us*

- Every number is a **verified government/ABS-derived median** (NSW Valuer General, VIC DTP, data.sa.gov.au, ABS SA2) — not portal estimates. Methodology is on the page.
- Tables are **CC BY 4.0** — free to republish with attribution + link.
- **Same-day custom cuts**: any state, region or price band on request. (This is the real hook for follow-up coverage — say yes to every request, fast.)

## Rules of engagement

- One outlet, one journalist at a time; 48h before pitching a second byline at the same masthead.
- Never attach files. Never follow up more than once (a single "any interest?" at day 4).
- Log every send in the table below; a "yes" to a custom cut is the win condition — turn those around same-day.
- Quarterly repeatability: re-run `scripts/seo/winter-2026-report-data.ts` each season (spring edition ~1 Sep) and pitch the *delta* ("Adelaide lost its last sub-$700k suburb this quarter").
